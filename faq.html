What are NBR extensions and how do they differ from other extensions?

Natural Beaded Rows (NBR) extensions are a premium extension method that uses beading and hand-stitching techniques to attach hair extensions. Unlike tape or glue extensions, NBR causes minimal damage, allows for natural movement, and creates a seamless, comfortable result. They're ideal for adding both length and volume and can last longer between maintenance appointments.

We recommend scheduling a consultation first to determine if NBR extensions are right for your hair type and goals.

A typical balayage service takes approximately 3 hours. This includes consultation, application, processing time, shampooing, toning, and styling. For first-time balayage clients or color corrections, the appointment may take longer. We recommend booking a consultation first if you're unsure.
